Instructions
      Use 'w','s','a','d' keys to navigate forward, backward, left and right respectively.
      Use SpaceBar to fly up and 'v' to fly down.
      Interactive objects will glow when the blue dot at the center of the screen hovers over them, press 'h' to interact with them.
      Press 'n' to start the animation sequence, which opens the door.
      Once you start moving towards the open door, you are taken inside.
      Once inside, use keys for navigation and interaction as specified above.


Please use Google Chrome for best experience.
